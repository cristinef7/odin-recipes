Halo-halo

This is Halo-halo.

Halo-halo means mixed together or variation.

Why do Filipinos love halo-halo?

Halo-Halo translated from Tagalog/Filipino literally means "mix-mix." Halo-halo is a favorite Filipino dessert or snack because it is cold and refreshing, perfect for beating the tropical heat that exists almost year round in the Philippines.

Ingredients:

  • ice
  • milk
  • ube halaya
  • brown sugar
  • shaved ice
  • mango
  • condensed milk
  • jackfruit
  • nata de coco
  • macapuno
  • ube ice cream
  • banana
  • beans

Steps

  1. Prepare tall glass
  2. Put shaved ice in glass
  3. Pour milk on shaved ice
  4. Put all the other ingredients on top of it
  5. Enjoy
